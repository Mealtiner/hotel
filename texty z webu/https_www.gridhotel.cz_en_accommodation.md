Rooms
The GRID HOTEL offers accommodation in 64 rooms in the categories of Standard , Superior , Superior Plus, Suite and Suite Plus .
All have comfortable furnishings, individually adjustable air conditioning, TV, Wi-Fi, work desk, minibar, blackout curtains, bathtub or shower.
Superior Plus, Suite and Suite Plus rooms also have terraces and a remarkable and unforgettable view of the racetrack and the surrounding area.
Rooms at the GRID Hotel are also suitable for handicapped guests.

Room equipment
-
Standard
All rooms meet all the parameters of the European standard ****.
In addition to comfortable equipment, TWIN or DOUBLE beds, there is individually adjustable air conditioning, a 40″ TV with HDMI input, Wi-Fi connection and a work desk.
The room has a security card lock system, room safe, full blackout option, direct dial telephone, minibar, shower, cosmetic mirror, hairdryer. Guests are provided with high-quality hotel cosmetics, shoe cleaning and sewing supplies.
On request, an extra pillow (or health pillow), an ironing board with an iron are available for free.
For a fee, guests can use a tea and coffee set, hotel bathrobe and slippers, and hygiene products.
-
Superior
Superior rooms have a view of the centre of action on the Brno Circuit and the surrounding forests.
Daily replenishment of the coffee and tea set, hotel robes, slippers and mineral water in rooms are provided to guests for free.
In addition to comfortable furnishings, TWIN or DOUBLE beds, guests will enjoy individually-controlled air-conditioning, a 40” TV with an HDMI port, a Wi-Fi connection and a desk.
Rooms have a security card lock system, a safe, blackout curtains, a telephone with a direct outside line, mini-bar, shower, makeup mirror and hairdryer. High-quality hotel cosmetics, shoe-shining products and sewing kits are available to guests.
On request, an extra pillow (or health pillow), an ironing board with an iron are available for free.
-
Superior Plus

Daily replenishment of the coffee and tea set, hotel robes, slippers and mineral water in rooms are provided to guests for free.
In addition to comfortable furnishings, TWIN or DOUBLE beds, guests will enjoy individually-controlled air-conditioning, a 40” TV with an HDMI port, a Wi-Fi connection and a desk.

On request, an extra pillow (or health pillow), an ironing board with an iron are available for free.

Suite and Suite Plus
Premium accommodation with the best view of the town, racetrack and paddock (parking lot of racing vehicles).
Hotel suites have a bedroom with a king bed, a living room and an overall area of 47−59 m², along with a luxurious 47 m² terrace.
Each room is furnished with a 43” TV with an HDMI port, as well as with two telephones with a directly outside line.
Guests can naturally expect the daily replenishment of the coffee and tea set, hotel robes, slippers and mineral water in their room. In addition to comfortable furnishings, guests will enjoy individually-controlled air-conditioning, a Wi-Fi connection and a desk.
