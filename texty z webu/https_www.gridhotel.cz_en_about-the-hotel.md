About the hotel
GRID HOTEL offers accommodation directly within the Masaryk Circuit in Brno.
GRID Hotel is a certified **** hotel. Your start to a new day will be made more pleasant by its hotel restaurant offerings. The GRID Club , hotel lobby, or the nearby PADDOCK Restaurant with a summer terrace will, on the other hand, be available to you throughout the rest of the day during the season.
Of course, there is Wi-Fi, parking for cars and buses, individually adjustable air conditioning, hotel services, activities within the Automotodrom, catering, and organizational support for corporate and large events.
