Getting here?
By car
- Arriving on the D1 motorway from Prague
Use exit 182 Kývalka and follow the road signs for “Grand Prix” and “Paddock” and subsequently the instructions given by the police and organisers.
- Arriving on the D1 motorway from Brno
Use exit 182 Kývalka and follow the signs for “Grand Prix” and “Paddock” and subsequently the instructions given by the police and organisers.
- Arriving from Brno – not on the D1 motorway
Class II road 602 from Brno-Bosonohy (runs parallel to the D1 motorway). Follow the “Autodrom” and “Grand Prix” road signs and subsequently the instructions given by the police and organisers.
- Arriving from Prague – not on the D1 motorway
Take the I/23 to Kývalka, continue along the II/602 and follow the signs for “Grand Prix” and “Paddock” and subsequently the instructions given by the police and organisers.
Public transport
There is a fixed bus service (no. 402) running to the Automotodrom from Starý Lískovec (to get to Starý Lískovec from the main train station take the no. 8 tram). The timetable for the 402 service can be downloaded here .
During international meetings, a special free bus service (no. 400) runs from Mendlovo náměstí (to get to Mendlovo náměstí from the main train station take the no. 1 tram towards Bystrc). The timetable can be downloaded here .
By plane
Brno International Airport
Timetable of scheduled flights ( link to www.airport-brno.cz )
Transport from Brno International Airport
Distance to Automotodrom Brno: 25 km
Take the D1 motorway (direction Prague) and turn off at exit 182 Kývalka ; follow the signs for “Grand Prix” and “Paddock”
Motorway coupon: CZ
Prague Airport – Ruzyně
International airport with a wide range of scheduled flights to destinations all over the world
Transport from Prague Airport
Distance to Automotodrom Brno: 210 km
Follow the Prague ring road onto the D1 motorway and turn off at exit 178 Ostrovačice ; follow the signs for “Grand Prix” and “Paddock”
Motorway coupon: CZ
Vienna Schwechat International Airport
Transport from Vienna International Airport:
Route 1
Take the A4 motorway (E85) to Bratislava and change onto the D2 motorway to Brno before finally changing onto the D1 motorway (direction Prague) and turning off at exit 182 Kývalka
Distance to Automotodrom Brno: 200 km
Motorway coupons: AT, SK, CZ
Route 2
Take the A4 motorway to Vienna and then follow the A23, S1, A5 and A7 roads – all heading to Brno, CZ. After crossing into the Czech Republic, continue along the I/52 (R52). After reaching Brno, turn onto the D1 motorway (direction Prague) and turn off at exit 182 Kývalka
Distance to Automotodrom Brno: 160 km
Motorway coupons: AT, CZ
Airport Bratislava (Slovakia)
Transport from Airport Bratislava
Distance to Automotodrom Brno: 150 km
Take the D2 motorway to Brno, change onto the D1 motorway (direction Prague) and turn off at exit 182 Kývalka
