The Grid Hotel offers accommodation directly on the grounds of the Masaryk Circuit in Brno.
Choose from 60 highly comfortable rooms and 4 suites featuring European 4-star quality.
Start your day with a delicious breakfast at the GRID Buffet .
The GRID Club and nearby GRID Restaurant with a summer terrace are open throughout the day.
Enjoy Wi-Fi internet connections, a parking lot for passenger cars and buses, individually controlled air-conditioning, hotel services, sports action at the racetrack, catering and organisational support for corporate and large events.
