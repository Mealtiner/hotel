Hotel Restaurant
Start your day with a pleasant breakfast, which is prepared for you every day from 7:00 to 10:00.
You can look forward to a rich selection of fresh dishes served as both a cold and hot buffet in the modern and tastefully decorated setting of our hotel restaurant.
Lunch is served from 12:00 to 15:00 in the form of a daily menu.
You can enjoy dinner with us from 18:00 to 21:30 à la carte.

GRID Club
After a long day or at any time during it, you can head to the stylish space of the GRID Club .
Thanks to its location near the hotel reception, it is easily accessible for business or professional meetings. The GRID Club also serves as a pleasant place to relax. A wide selection of drinks and light snacks, along with comfortable seating on the summer terrace with a unique view of the center of the circuit, will surely enhance your experience at the GRID Club .
The GRID Club is open from 12:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
PADDOCK Restaurant
The restaurant is located directly within the Masaryk Circuitarea and combines a unique motorsport atmosphere.
PADDOCK Restaurant offers comfortable seating for up to 80 guests and dining in the form of a rich self-service buffet. Guests can choose from hot dishes of national and international cuisine. The offer also includes a salad bar and desserts.
The restaurant provides all-day dining.
The restaurant includes an outdoor terrace, from which it ispossible to partially observe the activity in the race vehicles’ parking area. This space is also ideal for hosting festive and social events (award ceremonies, birthday parties, barbecues, and more).
