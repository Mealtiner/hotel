Activities
Enjoy the racing atmosphere surrounding the Brno Circuit.
Guests at the Grid Hotel have the unique opportunity to take rides on powerful motorbikes and dexterous pit bike s on a special track mere metres from the large racetrack.
The racetrack also offers adrenaline activities, a complete list of which is available on the circuit’s website .
Polygon Brno
Take a safe driving lesson at the modern training centre Polygon Brno . You can find there four levels of skid school or special company courses based on individual requirements.
