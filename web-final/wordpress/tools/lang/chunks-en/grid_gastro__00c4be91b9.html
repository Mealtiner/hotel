Hotel Restaurant

Breakfast · Lunch · Dinner

Start your day with a rich breakfast served as a cold and hot buffet in our modern, tastefully appointed hotel restaurant. A daily menu during the day, à la carte dining with a view of the track in the evening.

  • Breakfast 7:00–10:00
  • Lunch (daily menu) 12:00–15:00
  • Dinner (à la carte) 18:00–21:30

PADDOCK Restaurant

Right within the circuit grounds

Located directly within the Masaryk Circuit area, with comfortable seating for up to 80 guests. A rich self-service buffet of national and international cuisine, a salad bar and desserts. The outdoor terrace offers a partial view of the paddock — ideal for social events too.

  • Capacity up to 80 guests
  • Buffet & salad bar
  • All-day dining

GRID Club

Open 12:00–24:00

  • Cocktail bar
  • Terrace with a view
  • Afterparties & race weekends
