The taste of the day, from breakfast to the last drink

From the breakfast buffet through the daily menu to evening à la carte dining — plus the stylish GRID Club and PADDOCK Restaurant right by the track. We will also take care of the catering for your wedding or celebration.

Hotel Restaurant

Breakfast · Lunch · Dinner

Start your day with a rich breakfast served as a cold and hot buffet in our modern, tastefully appointed hotel restaurant. A daily menu during the day, à la carte dining with a view of the track in the evening.

  • Breakfast 7:00–10:00
  • Lunch (daily menu) 12:00–15:00
  • Dinner (à la carte) 18:00–21:30

PADDOCK Restaurant

Right within the circuit grounds

Located directly within the Masaryk Circuit area, with comfortable seating for up to 80 guests. A rich self-service buffet of national and international cuisine, a salad bar and desserts. The outdoor terrace offers a partial view of the paddock — ideal for social events too.

  • Capacity up to 80 guests
  • Buffet & salad bar
  • All-day dining

GRID Club

Open 12:00–24:00

Stylish premises near the hotel reception — ideal for business meetings as well as pure relaxation. A wide selection of drinks and light snacks, plus comfortable seating on the summer terrace overlooking the centre of the circuit.

  • Cocktail bar
  • Terrace with a view
  • Afterparties & race weekends
Service · Catering

  • Tailor-made menu and buffet
