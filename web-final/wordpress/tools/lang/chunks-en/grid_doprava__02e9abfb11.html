How to get here?

You will find GRID HOTEL right inside the Autodrom Brno grounds at Ostrovačická 936/65, Masaryk Circuit, 641 00 Brno – Žebětín. On arrival, follow the road signs „Grand Prix" and „Paddock".

Get directions →Shuttle bus: +420 775 778 718

By car

On the D1 motorway from PragueUse exit 178 – Ostrovačice, then follow the Grand Prix and Paddock road signs.
On the D1 motorway from BrnoUse exit 182 Kývalka, then follow the „Grand Prix" or „Paddock" signs. Coming from Olomouc, Bratislava or Vienna, also use exit 182 Kývalka.
From Brno avoiding the D1 motorwayTake class III road 3842 from Brno-Žebětín. Signposted Autodrom and Grand Prix.
From Prague avoiding the D1 motorwayTake the I/23 to Kývalka, then continue along the II/602 – follow the „Grand Prix" and „Paddock" signs.

By public transport

Bus 402A regular service from Starý Lískovec (from the main train station, take tram 8 towards Starý Lískovec).
Bus 400 · freeDuring international events, a special free service runs from Mendlovo náměstí (from the main train station, take tram 1 towards Bystrc). The timetable is published ahead of each event.
