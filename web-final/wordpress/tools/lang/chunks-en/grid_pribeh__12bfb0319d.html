The only hotel
inside the track.

GRID HOTEL doesn't stand beside the track. It stands right inside the Automotodrom Brno grounds at Ostrovačická 65 — just metres from the famous Masaryk Circuit.

From your room window and the terrace you overlook the heart of the circuit and the surrounding forests. You wake up to the sound of engines, head to breakfast with a view of the track and wind down in the evening at the restaurant or the GRID Club. It's the only place in the Czech Republic where you sleep in the middle of a racing legend.
