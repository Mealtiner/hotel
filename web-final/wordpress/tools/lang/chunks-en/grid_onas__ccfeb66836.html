About the hotel

A hotel right
on the circuit grounds

GRID HOTEL offers accommodation directly within the grounds of the Masaryk Circuit in Brno — the only hotel inside Automotodrom Brno.

GRID Hotel is a certified **** hotel. Your start to a new day will be made all the more pleasant by the offerings of the hotel restaurant. The GRID Club, the hotel lobby or the nearby PADDOCK Restaurant with its summer terrace are at your disposal throughout the rest of the day during the season.

Of course, there is Wi-Fi, parking for cars and buses, individually adjustable air conditioning, hotel services, activities within the Automotodrom, catering, and organizational support for corporate and large-scale events.

Why GRID

Circuit adrenaline and forest calm in one place

We are not a hotel near the track — we stand right inside the grounds of the famous Masaryk Circuit, just steps from the paddock and the start–finish straight, surrounded by Brno's forests. In the morning you wake up to the buzz of a race weekend; in the evening you fall asleep in perfect peace.

Throughout the year we are the home base of motorsport fans during MotoGP, endurance races and track days, as well as the venue for corporate events, weddings and a quiet getaway. Add to that our own dining, trackside experiences and complete organizational support.
